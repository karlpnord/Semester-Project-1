Young Stars Club

We have the Young Stars Club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you'll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

During the school holidays we run holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.
