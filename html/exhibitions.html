Exhibitions

Biology and Medicine

Experience the transformative power of medicine as we delve into the exhibition, unveiling the remarkable strides that have extended human lifespans and enhanced quality of life. Marvel at the historical milestones, including the game-changing revelation of penicillin and the groundbreaking achievement of the first successful heart transplant, marking monumental leaps forward in medical science.

Learn more

Evolution

Delve into the timeless quest of humanity's origins as both philosophers and scientists grapple with the profound questions of our existence. Uncover the secrets of our genesis, unearthing the fascinating narrative of how we came to be. Embark on a captivating journey through the annals of time, guided by Darwin's revolutionary theory of evolution, which provides a lens to explore and comprehend our place in this vast world.

Learn more

Ecology

Embark on a thought-provoking expedition through our museum's ecology section, where we confront the imminent perils that loom before us. Unveil the stark dangers of global warming, the alarming specter of species extinction, and the pervasive menace of pollution. However, amidst this stark reality, we shine a beacon of hope, emphasizing the transformative power of collective action.

Learn more

Cosmology

Embark on an awe-inspiring cosmic journey as we unveil the captivating wonders of "The Sky Above Us" exhibition. Prepare to be mesmerized as we delve into the enigmatic realm of the night sky, unlocking the secrets of the universe that envelops us. Behold the celestial tapestry adorned with twinkling constellations and distant galaxies, inviting us to unravel their mysteries.

Learn more

Robotics and AI

Prepare to be enthralled as the future unfolds before your eyes. Engage with the cutting-edge technologies and come face-to-face with the remarkable Rob the Robot, a testament to the incredible advancements in humanoid robotics. Embrace the extraordinary opportunity to greet and even shake hands with this remarkable creation, bridging the gap between science fiction and reality.

Learn more
